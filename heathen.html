Hail and Welcome

Welcome to the digital hall of the New Zealand Heathen Council. We are a network of kindreds, hearths, and solitary practitioners dedicated to the historical traditions, lore, and practices of old Norse heathenry.

Whether you are drawn to the wisdom of the High One, the strength of the Thunderer, or the deep roots of the World Tree, you will find fellowship and knowledge here.

ᛟ ᚹ ᚫ

"Cattle die, kindred die, Every man is mortal: But the good name never dies Of one who has done well."
- Hávamál

Our Purpose

The New Zealand Heathen Council exists to foster community, ensure the accurate sharing of historical lore, and provide a unified voice for practitioners operating under New Zealand law.

Our Values

  • Frith: Maintaining peace and strong bonds within our communities.
  • Hospitality: Welcoming those who seek knowledge with an open mind.
  • Historical Grounding: Rooting our rituals in primary sources.

Upcoming Rites & Moots

Our seasonal celebrations are inverted to align with the Southern Hemisphere tides.

Yule (Midwinter): June 20th - 22nd

Sigrblót (First Day of Summer): September 21st - 23rd

Midsomar (Midsummer): December 20th - 22nd

NorseWood Festival: Waitangi Weekend

Winter Night (First Day of Winter): March 20th - 22nd
